our college

With 1.2 billion people living in uninhabitable shacks in some of the world’s worst slums, life without secure housing is a grim reality. Without a secure home, these families lack dignity and hope for the future. That’s why we founded World Housing, a non-profit social enterprise that provides homes with lockable doors for those living in the developing world’s urban slums. Our vision is simple—a home for everyone.
