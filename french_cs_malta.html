NEMESIS is looking for a French Customer Support Agent. You will be a part of our Customer Support Department in Malta (30 people). You will support one of our key clients with their international speaking players across their platforms.





The role also requires that:



• Assisting players via chat and email.
• Providing top notch level of customer satisfaction to players.
• Maintaining customers focus and responding to customers queries in accordance to company guidelines.
• Supporting and guiding customers with all requests and escalating and following up where necessary.
• Promoting the brands’ values and products.
• Ensuring professional working relationships with customers and colleagues
